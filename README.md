# 🏗️ SCL Contracts
**Premium Construction & Specialty Contracting Company**

SCL Contracts is a leading construction and specialty services company delivering high-quality projects across India. With a strong foundation in innovation, precision, and craftsmanship, we specialize in creating durable and aesthetically superior spaces.

---

## 🚀 About

SCL Contracts brings over a decade of expertise in construction, offering end-to-end solutions across multiple service verticals. The company operates with a client-first philosophy, ensuring excellence, reliability, and long-term value in every project.

A subsidiary of **Siddhika Coatings Limited**, SCL focuses on premium construction solutions and high-end facade works.
